Diode I-V Characteristics

Connect the Diode from CH3 to Ground.
Connect a 1k resistor from PV1 to CH3

PV1 is varied in steps. For each step the current is
calculated from  the difference between voltages
at PV1 and CH3.
It is plotted against voltage across the diode, at CH3.
Acquired plots can be selectively displayed or deleted.
